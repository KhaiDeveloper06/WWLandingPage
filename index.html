Interactive Field Manual

1

Locate the floating menu icon (bottom right). Drag it to a comfortable spot on your screen, then hover over it to expand your toolset.

2

As soon as a game starts, click "Scan Role". This loads the current lobby's player icons into your tracker for lightning-fast noting.

3

After the game ends, click "Reset Board". This wipes your chat and vote data clean so you are immediately ready for the next lobby.

4

Track the casualties. The matrix automatically visualizes dead players, providing better readability so you can focus entirely on the surviving suspects.

Current System Flaws (V1.0.1)

  • Manual "Scan Role" required at the start of each match to grab data.
  • Manual "Reset Board" required before new matches to wipe old data.
  • Switching Day/Night tabs as a Werewolf duplicates the chat counter.
  • Chat counter currently tracks Jailer & Dead chats, which is in fact a flaw, but it ignores special channels (Instigator, Alpha, Sect Leader).

Dev Notes & Future Roadmap

Why the chat counter bug persists: Dead chats aren't tactically important anymore, and Werewolves already know who their team is (so they don't need the counter to hunt). However, a patch fixing the duplicate counting and night channels is planned for the future.
