Privacy Policy

Transparency is critical when it comes to gaming tools. The short version? Wolvesville Wizard does not track you, does not collect your personal information, and does not send any game data to external servers.

This tool was built to give you a tactical edge, not to harvest your data. Everything happens entirely on your own device.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We collect absolutely nothing. There are no analytics trackers, no background telemetry, and no hidden scripts. The extension reads the public chat and voting elements directly from your browser screen solely to update your local overlay in real time.

2. Why We Need the "Storage" Permission

When you install the extension, Chrome will notify you that it requires "storage" permissions. We use your browser's secure local storage exclusively to save your custom player notes, tactical layout preferences, and the current state of the matrix. This ensures your data survives if you accidentally refresh the page during a match. None of this data ever leaves your computer.

3. Third-Party Access

Because we do not collect any data, we have absolutely nothing to share, sell, or transfer to third-party services, advertising networks, or corporate entities.
